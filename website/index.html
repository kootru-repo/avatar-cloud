Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Kootru LLC ("Kootru," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all individuals who interact with our services. This Privacy Policy describes our practices concerning data handling in connection with our live custom AI avatar services (the "Services").

By accessing our website or engaging our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Our Role as a Service Provider

Kootru operates exclusively as a technology service provider to business clients ("Clients"). We provide customized AI avatar solutions that are deployed and controlled by our Clients for their own business purposes.

Critical Distinction: Kootru does not collect, store, process, or distribute any personal data from end users who interact with AI avatars deployed by our Clients. Any data exchanged during avatar interactions is solely between the end user and the Client's systems.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Through our AI avatar interface and technology, Kootru does not collect, retain, or have access to:

  • Personal identifying information of end users
  • Conversation logs, transcripts, or recordings
  • Voice data, biometric data, or facial recognition data
  • Behavioral data, usage patterns, or interaction history
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, or geolocation data of end users
  • Any data input by end users during avatar interactions

4. Data Processing on Behalf of Clients

When providing customization and configuration services to our Clients, we process only the information expressly provided to us by the Client for the purpose of building and deploying their custom avatar solution. This may include:

  • Brand assets (logos, color schemes, style guidelines)
  • Knowledge base content provided by the Client
  • Voice samples or persona specifications authorized by the Client
  • Integration credentials and technical configurations

All Client-provided data is used exclusively for the purpose of delivering the contracted Services and is handled in accordance with our Data Processing Agreement with each Client.

5. Client Responsibilities

Our Clients, as data controllers for their deployed avatar solutions, bear sole responsibility for:

  • Compliance with all applicable data protection laws and regulations (including GDPR, CCPA, and other jurisdictional requirements)
  • Providing appropriate privacy notices to their end users
  • Obtaining necessary consents for data collection and processing
  • Handling, storing, and securing any data collected through their avatar deployment
  • Responding to data subject access requests and other privacy-related inquiries from their end users

6. Website Data Collection

When you visit kootru.com or submit inquiries through our contact form, we may collect:

  • Contact information you voluntarily provide (name, email, company name)
  • Inquiry details and message content
  • Basic analytics data (page views, referral sources) via privacy-respecting analytics

This information is used solely for responding to your inquiries, providing requested information about our Services, and improving our website experience.

7. Data Security

Kootru implements industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect any data within our control, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection practices

8. Data Retention

Client-provided configuration data is retained only for the duration of the service relationship plus any legally required retention period. Upon termination of services, Client data is securely deleted within thirty (30) days unless otherwise required by law or contractual obligation.

9. Third-Party Disclosure

Kootru does not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise distribute any data to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in delivering our Services, under strict contractual confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Kootru, our Clients, or others

10. International Data Transfers

If Client-provided data is transferred across international borders, Kootru ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data, including access, correction, deletion, and portability. For any data held by Kootru directly (such as contact form submissions), please contact us using the information below. For data collected through a Client's deployed avatar, please contact that Client directly.

12. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. Kootru does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Clients deploying avatar solutions must ensure compliance with applicable laws regarding minors.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our Services following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
